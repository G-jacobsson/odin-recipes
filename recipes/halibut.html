Heavenly Halibut

Description

This recipe makes the best halibut recipe you've ever tasted.

You will find yourself making this again and again.

Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ cup butter, softened
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 3 tablespoons chopped green onions
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 dash hot pepper sauce
  • 2 pounds skinless halibut fillets

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven broiler. Grease a baking dish.
  2. In a bowl, mix the Parmesan cheese, butter,

    3. mayonnaise, lemon juice, green onions, salt and

    hot pepper sauce.

  3. Arrange the halibut fillets in the prepared

    4. baking dish.

  4. Broil halibut fillets 8 minutes in the prepared

    5. oven, or until easily flaked with a fork. Spread

    with the Parmesan cheese mixture, and continue

    broiling 2 minutes, or until topping is bubbly and

    lightly browned.

  5. Serve with rice and salad of your choice.
